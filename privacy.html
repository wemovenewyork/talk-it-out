Privacy Policy

Effective April 5, 2026 · Plain language version

The short version

Your form data and submissions belong to you. Your employer cannot access your data through Talk It Out. Voice recordings are used only for live transcription and are never stored. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Talk It Out service. This includes the following:

  • Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Form content and answers you provide during the filing process.
  • Form images you upload for field extraction — deleted within 24 hours.
  • Device and browser information for offline functionality and app performance.
  • Aggregate anonymized usage data to improve the service.

What we do not collect

We do not sell your data to any third party under any circumstances.

We do not share individual form submissions with your employer, their representatives, or any management personnel.

We do not store voice recordings. Audio is processed live for transcription and immediately discarded — never saved to our servers.

We do not use your form content, answers, or voice input to train AI models.

Data we collect and how long we keep it

Data type Purpose Kept for
Account information Name, email, org code Until account deleted
Form submissions Your completed filings and answers Until you delete them
Voice recordings Live transcription only Not stored — discarded immediately
Form images Field extraction 24 hours maximum
E-signatures Document signing Lifetime of the submission
Usage analytics App performance and improvement 90 days rolling
Error logs Debugging and reliability 30 days

Your rights

Request a complete copy of all data associated with your account at any time by emailing privacy@talkitout.app.

Permanently delete your account and all associated data from the profile screen or by emailing privacy@talkitout.app with subject "Data Deletion Request".

Correct or update any inaccurate personal information directly in the app profile screen.

Opt out of all non-essential communications at any time from notification preferences in the app.

California residents — full CCPA rights apply. You have the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not sell data.

EU and UK residents have rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Contact privacy@talkitout.app for any GDPR requests.

Third parties and sub-processors

We use a limited number of third party services to operate Talk It Out. All sub-processors are bound by data processing agreements and may only use your data to provide their specific service.

  • Anthropic — AI text processing and form field extraction via Claude API.
  • Standard cloud hosting providers for infrastructure.
  • Email delivery service for notification emails.

We do not use advertising networks, marketing trackers, social media pixels, or analytics services that share data with third parties. A complete list of sub-processors is available on request at privacy@talkitout.app.

How we protect your data

We take security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3.
  • Form submissions and personal data are encrypted at rest.
  • E-signatures are stored as encrypted base64 data.
  • We conduct regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

Contact us

We take privacy questions seriously and respond to all inquiries within 5 business days.
